Site updates

Changes to content, design and functionality — newest first. Each entry is one meaningful unit of work; related fixes are grouped rather than listed individually.

April 2026

27 Apr 2026 Performance

Images, preloading and script loading optimised

The logo was resized from 512 × 512 px to 192 × 192 px and converted from PNG (55 KB) to lossless WebP (9 KB), reducing the largest-contentful-paint asset by 84 % on every page. A preload hint was added to the <head> of every page so the browser begins fetching the logo before body parsing reaches it, and a preconnect hint for Google Tag Manager eliminates the ~200 ms DNS and handshake cost for analytics. The navigation script gained a defer attribute, and an orphaned image file with no references was removed.

27 Apr 2026 UX

Eleven UX and accessibility improvements

Cards no longer lift on hover — the lift effect was a false affordance implying every card was a link. The topics dropdown gained an open/close animation, caret rotation, Escape-key handling, and proper ARIA attributes (aria-expanded, aria-haspopup, aria-hidden). Other changes: :focus-visible outlines across all interactive elements; the header shrinks after 80 px of scroll; the scroll button now returns to the top of the page rather than to the previous section; active-page links are highlighted; language-switch buttons have descriptive title attributes; and all card titles were converted from <div> to semantic <h3> across all pages.

26 Apr 2026 Analytics

GA4 with Consent Mode v2 and satisfaction signals

Google Analytics 4 was installed with Consent Mode v2: all storage permissions default to denied, and consent is updated via localStorage when granted. Satisfaction signals track scroll depth at 25 %, 50 % and 75 %, time on page at 30 s, 60 s and 120 s, internal navigation clicks, and quick-exit behaviour for visitors arriving from search engines.

26 Apr 2026 Content

New page: Swiss transfer pricing exposure (/summit/)

A standalone content page was published at /summit/ covering five structural exposure points in Swiss transfer pricing: the absence of codified arm's-length rules, audit-cycle risk, withholding tax on hidden distributions, cooperation duties beyond documentation, and the limits of interquartile range compliance. The page also outlines three pre-audit actions and a referral proposition for external advisers handling mandates with a Swiss side.

25 Apr 2026 Content

Four content corrections on DE and EN homepages

The positioning statement "Wir verteidigen, wir korrigieren, wir vertreten." / "We defend, we correct, we represent." was added as a visible sentence in the hero section on both homepages. A bullet covering Swiss tax proceedings without a German nexus was added to the "typically a fit" list. "Begleitung des MAP" was corrected to "Führung des MAP" on the German dispute resolution page. Swiss-German orthography was normalised throughout: ß replaced with ss on all German pages.

24 Apr 2026 Design

Primary red adjusted to #ff230a

The brand red was refined from #ff3600 to #ff230a — a slightly cooler tone that reads more cleanly at small sizes and holds sufficient contrast against the dark header background. The change propagates through all card backgrounds, button fills and link colours via a single CSS custom property.

23 Apr 2026 Design

Mobile navigation and puzzle-piece footer

A hamburger menu was added for viewports below 760 px, with a three-bar-to-X animation and click-outside dismissal. The footer was rebuilt as a three-panel puzzle layout: connecting tabs and notches visually link the Zurich office, Hamburg office and Quantera Global network tiles into a single unit. Topic pages, the IncaMail contact card and a scroll-to-previous-section button were also added at this point.

22 Apr 2026 Redesign

2026 redesign: light theme, design system, legal pages

The site moved from a dark theme to a light #f2f2f2 base, with red-filled cards, a sticky dark header and a structured single-page layout for both homepages. A CSS custom-property design system was introduced, and the Quantera Global network tile was added to every footer. The Impressum and Datenschutzerklärung were populated with complete legal content.

17 Apr 2026 UX

Dropdown navigation and encrypted contact standardised

The topics dropdown was rebuilt from a native <details> element to a JS-controlled button toggle, fixing rendering inconsistencies across browsers where the native element ignored custom styling. The IncaMail encrypted-message button was standardised sitewide with a consistent post-submit note explaining file attachment support and message confirmation download.

15 Apr 2026 Content

English section launched

The full English-language section went live: homepage (/en.html) and four topic pages covering permanent establishments and profit allocation, transfer pricing and adjustments, cross-border adjustments, and dispute resolution DE–CH. All pages include hreflang annotations linking the German and English counterparts for search engines.

14 Apr 2026 Launch

oi.tax launched

oi.tax launched as a static GitHub Pages site with German homepage, sticky header, CSS design system and custom domain configuration. Initial content covered bilateral correction coordination DE↔CH as the standalone service, with permanent establishment disputes, transfer pricing adjustments and cross-border proceedings as the three main triggers.
